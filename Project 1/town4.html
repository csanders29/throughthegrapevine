The smell of baked bread floats through the air as you pass open bakery doors. The store is full of people browsing the baked goods.
“Oh these look good! Are they a new creation?”

“Yes! I made them with some boysenberries i’ve been growing in the garden, but I really wish I had some inanis berries on hand!”

“You always say that when you make something new!”

“Of course! Inanis berries are said to add years on your life! I could use a couple more, you know! I’m getting old after all!”

“Its too bad, they say inanis berries only grow in Rima forest! It’s almost as if that place is always night with how dark it is in there. It doesn’t help that inanis berries are just as dark. You can’t see anything in there!"
