The entrance of the cave is nothing more than a dot behind you. You can’t see anything.
You shuffle forward slowly.

You kick up a few rocks but you haven’t fallen yet, so you keep going.

You see a dot of light in the distance up ahead.
You turn around and you can’t see the entrance behind you anymore.
Maybe you’ve gone in circles?

You walk forward towards the light.

You reach the light source, to find a flower, basking in the light filtering through a crack in the ceiling of the cave. You look around the place but there doesn’t seem to be any other way to go except the way you came.
