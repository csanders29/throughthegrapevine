You shove past a particularly large crowd gathered around a loud salesman.
“Have a look folks! Today we have this special armor in stock! Just as impenetrable as a klapa, if not more!”

“That salesman’s a joke! Everyone knows a klapa isn’t breakable.”
“Oh, but have you heard? They say that if you can break a klapa open, drinking the water inside it can make you ten times stronger!”

“That’s just a legend! No one’s even heard of someone cracking one, let alone drinking from one!”
“But I’ve heard that if you take two klapas and smash them together they’d crack.”

“Who’s got the time for that? Walking through Silva forest is such a hazard, you could get killed from having one of those things falling on your head!”
