You pass the cave entrance and keep moving forward.
The trail stretches on to the top of the mountain.

The air gets thinner as you keep walking. The trail gets steeper and steeper.
As you finally reach the top, you see nothing but dirt, rocks, and some sparse shrubbery. You hear a noise from behind you.
You turn around. Its a goat. It runs you off the mountain.

You’ve managed to escape the goat somehow. You’re back at the base of the mountain.
