You, the player, have a sick mother who will die soon, unless there's a miracle. The doctor says there's no medicine that can cure her.
With no options, you seek to make a legendary elixir that is said to cure anything. You must scour the country and find ingredients for the elixir.

You're not sure how to find these ingredients, so take advantage of the towns to learn about them!
Hurry before it's too late!
